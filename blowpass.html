Design Concept for Blowpass app

Concept

2016 Professional Salon Industry hair care study from Professional Consultants and Resources, a leading salon industry strategic consultant and data source reported total US salon services and sale growth increased by 3% to 62 billion. Some of the main growth drivers were basic cuts, color and styling. With the increasing pace of urban lifestyle, along with women looking for affordable and fast hair care, there is an increasing dependency on these professional services which provides quality hair styling within a frame of time.


Style/Blowout bars are saloons which runs in the concept of ordering a hairstyle from a menu, accompanied by complimentary drinks. These hairstyles are done within 15-30 minutes , are luxurious and affordable , prices falling between $35-$50


With the increase in the number and demand of style saloons, there is an opportunity to build an app which can allow the user to find a style bar anywhere they are, read the reviews and help them make fast appointments.

UX Methodologies

  • User Research
  • Ideation
  • Persona creation
  • Wireframing
  • Usability Testing

User Research

For user research, I reached out to my friends and family, I asked them questions on their hairstyling / blow-drying frequency. I also performed contextual inquiry and visited some of the style bars near my apartment and spoke to managers and stylists.

User Persona

Usability Testing and Key Finding

Usability testing was done by three women, age ranging from 25-31. Their blowout /styling frequency ranges from once a week to 3 times a week. They enjoyed the concept of a one stop shop for style bars. Navigation between features was easy. 2 out of 3 users wanted a feature where they have a card in file for their accounts and pay through the app for their services and wanted a frequency based point system which could also be used to pay for services.

Visual Design

Purple represents Luxury , extravagance , power, magic and pink represents friendship, affection ,inner peace and approachability. Blowpass would want to exude a luxurious yet approachable brand in itself to get more customers looking for a service which makes them feel confident , luxurious , powerful and beautiful while being budget and time friendly.The visual assets were created by me using Sketch software.
