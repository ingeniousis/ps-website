Redesigning StepUp app

Project Needs

Redesign StepUp app to increase customer retention, make it more visually appealing and introduce new features with minimum changes to the existing app.

UX Methodologies

  • Comparative/Competitive Analysis
  • Affinity diagrams
  • Persona creation
  • Task Analysis
  • Concept Map
  • Userflow
  • Wireframing
  • Prototype testing in Invision

Software Used

  • Sketch
  • Invision
  • Keynote Presentation

Research

The StepUp team had given me access to their Facebook Analytics page from which the primary research was done. For competitive analysis two Pedometer ++ and Fitbit were main considerations as per the request of the Stakeholder.

Usability Testing

7 users were asked to do usability testing throughout the whole process,4 users were selected by the stakeholders ,3 users were selected from a questionnaire which was sent out. 86 % users i.e. 6/7 were apprehensive about the Facebook login which the app recommends.100% users found the app functionally sound but visually poor. The idea of active bot and lazy bot wasn’t understood by 43% users (These were the first-time users of the app). Nudge functionality was perceived as being the same as "poke" in Facebook (negative perception). The step history page according to 71% users was not visually appealing but did the job

The overall brand colors were perceived to be clinical

The color reminds me of hospital gowns - Tess O.

The notification feature, and cheer, taunt and nudge feature for users who have not logged in through Facebook seemed odd

I don’t want to cheer an imaginary friend - Jeremiah G.

Report Findings

From my usability testing, I reported back to the stakeholders the key areas of concern after which the Stakeholders and I discussed the scope of change.

Proto Persona

From the pain points collected, wants and desires, and also the layout of the Affinity Diagram I was able to create 3 personas and narrow it down to 1. This main persona is the target user for StepUp.

Initial Ideations

Visuals

From our usability testing, it was found that the present colors of StepUp were considered “Clinical” in nature. Users wanted more vibrant colors which would represent motivation and hope. After going through a few color pallets we choose to use a shade of Blue and a darker shade of pink as primary colors. Orange represents enthusiasm, motivation, determination and encouragement while blue clams the mind and body. These were the emotions that I wanted the StepUp app to communicate visually. To avoid any further changes, it was decided that the fonts in the app will be the default SF Pro Text.

Next Steps

For the next phase of the project we will concentrate on more User testing, building badges which motivates people and move over to redesigning the screens for people who login via Facebook, after which we will move to designing the app for apple watch.
